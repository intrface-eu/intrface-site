# About Page PRD

## Page Name
About Page

## Goal
Explain who Intrface is, what it builds, and the philosophy connecting its products, systems, and long-term platform direction.

## Primary Audience
- Partners
- Future hires
- Curious technical visitors
- Network contacts evaluating Intrface as a company

## Source Documents / Repos
- Intrface site master PRD
- Homepage PRD
- Product repo context from AOC, Voyager, and Funda

## Key Message
Intrface builds AI-native platforms, products, and systems with a focus on clarity, intelligence, and durable workflow design.

## Page Job To Be Done
- define who Intrface is
- explain what kinds of products it builds
- communicate the company thesis and working principles
- support trust and future collaboration

## Required Sections
- Intro / who we are
- What we build
- How we work / principles
- Product ecosystem relationship
- Optional future vision / closing CTA

## Required CTAs
- View Projects
- Contact Intrface

## Must-Avoid Misrepresentations
- Do not present Intrface as a generic service agency if product-building is the core story
- Do not write abstract manifesto copy without grounding in actual products

## Content Model Needs
- company profile
- principles list
- ecosystem narrative blocks
- CTA links

## Design Notes
- Use Google Sans Flex as the primary typeface
- The page should feel calm, credible, and thesis-driven
- Strong typography and narrative pacing matter more than decorative visuals

## Acceptance Criteria
- Visitors understand what Intrface is and why its products belong together
- The page supports trust and future collaboration without overexplaining
