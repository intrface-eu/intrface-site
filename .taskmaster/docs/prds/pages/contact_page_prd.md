# Contact Page PRD

## Page Name
Contact Page

## Goal
Provide a clean, trustworthy, low-friction path for visitors to reach Intrface for partnerships, product interest, collaboration, or related inquiries.

## Primary Audience
- Potential partners
- Interested customers
- Collaborators
- Talent / network contacts

## Source Documents / Repos
- Intrface site master PRD
- Homepage PRD
- Product PRDs

## Key Message
Intrface is open to meaningful product, partnership, and platform conversations.

## Page Job To Be Done
- make reaching out easy
- clarify what kinds of conversations are relevant
- avoid clutter or generic contact-page filler

## Required Sections
- Intro / invitation
- Contact pathways or form
- What to reach out about
- Optional direct links to flagship products

## Required CTAs
- Send Inquiry
- Explore Projects

## Must-Avoid Misrepresentations
- Do not create unnecessary friction
- Do not make the page feel like a generic agency lead form if the company is product-led

## Content Model Needs
- contact methods or form config
- inquiry categories
- CTA links

## Design Notes
- Use Google Sans Flex as the primary typeface
- Keep the page simple, clear, and high-trust
- Prioritize form clarity and calm hierarchy

## Acceptance Criteria
- Visitors can understand how to reach out in seconds
- The page supports real inquiries without bloated content
